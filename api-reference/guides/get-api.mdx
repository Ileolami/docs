## Registration

<Note>
   **Please note that the API registration process is not optimized for mobile devices so you should access these pages on a desktop computer and browser**.
   Also Before being issued an API key you will have to agree to our terms of use. You can read that [here](https://www.themoviedb.org/documentation/api/terms-of-use).
</Note>
<Steps>
 <Step >
  Go to [TheMovieDB](https://www.themoviedb.org/)
 </Step>
 <Step>
    Click on `Join TMBD`
    <Frame>
     <img height="100" noZoom src="/images/tmbd.png"/>
    </Frame>
    ---
 </Step>

 <Step>
  Fill in your details
    <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/signup.png"/>
    </Frame>
    ---
 </Step>
 <Step>
   Verify if you are human by clicking on `Begin`
    <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/human-verfiy.png"/>
    </Frame>
    ---
 </Step>
 <Step>
   TMBD will send you a mail to confirm your email address. Click on `ACTIVATE My ACCOUNT`
    <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/activate.png"/>
    </Frame>
   <Accordion title="Did receive confirmation email?">
   If you don't get a mail, you can request a resend.
    <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/resend.png"/>
    </Frame>
   </Accordion>
   ---
 </Step>
   <Step>
    Once your email has been verfied, enter your username and password you used for registration
    <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/login.png"/>
    </Frame>
   </Step>
</Steps>

## Generate your API Key

<Steps>
  <Step>
  Once you've login, Click on profile(an icon with username's first letter)
    <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/userfirst.png"/>
    </Frame>
    ---
  </Step>
  <Step>
   Click on `Settings`
    <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/setting.png"/>
    </Frame>
    ---
  </Step>
  <Step>
   On the sidebar, scroll down and click on `API`
    <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/API.png"/>
    </Frame>
    ---
  </Step>
  <Step>
   Under `Request an API Key`, click on `click here` to generate an API key
     <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/click-here.png"/>
    </Frame>
    ---
  </Step>
  <Step>
   Choose the type of API key you want to register for and accept the term of use
    <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/type.png"/>
    </Frame>
    ---
  </Step>
  <Step>
   Fill in your application details and click on `submit`
    <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/application.png"/>
    </Frame>
    ---
  </Step>
  <Step>
   After a successful submission, you will be routed to another page. 
   Scroll down for your `API Read Access Token` and `api key`
     <Frame>
     <img height="200" noZoom src="/images/read-access.png"/>
    </Frame>
    <Warning>
    Don't share any of these details with anyone
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>
You can always copy your [API key ](https://www.themoviedb.org/settings/api) from your account settings page.
